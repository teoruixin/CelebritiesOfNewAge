Unearthing YouTube's Explosive Rise

As people around the world found themselves confined to their homes, many turned to online platforms not only as sources of diversion but also as creative outlets and means of connectivity. Content creation has now become one of the biggest industries, building the careers of many famous influencers and celebrities. The rise in unemployment during the pandemic has led to people turning towards content creation to support their lifestyles.

A Historical Look at YouTube's Category Shifts

Back when YouTube was founded, most of the top YouTubers were from the "Entertainment" and "Music" categories. The number of top channels creating "Music" content remain at a constant high until 2012, where it has been decreasing since then. The number of top channels creating "Entertainment" videos have fluctuated through the years. While the number of top channels have been high up until 2018, it faced a low in number of top channels in 2009. The number of top channels have also been on a decline since 2019.

From 2011-2012, there has been a spike in number of top channels creating "Gaming" videos which then decreased in following years. The number of top channels creating "People & Vlog" videos spiked in 2014. In 2019, there was a small spike in "People & Vlog" top youtube channels, which could be attributed to the COVID-19 pandemic.
